C2C EST. 2020

Coast to Coast Tutors was founded in 2020 on the premise of providing better quality tutoring to high schoolers at reasonable prices. Coast to Coast was created by Ethan Seder and Jake Libenson, two highly successful college students who felt they could offer superior tutoring services that were better priced and more personable towards students.

Coast to Coast Tutors offers a tutoring experience that allows students to excel, reach their full potential, and foster productive relationships with successful college students with proven track records. Coast to Coast Tutors employs top college students, with superb GPAs, extensive resumes, and great conversational skills to help make the best tutoring experience possible.

Coast to Coast tutors offers services across all disciplines from the sciences to the humanities. We are primarily focused on mentoring high school students, but also work with college students on a variety of subjects occassionally.

C2C Tutoring - The Founders

Ethan Seder and Jake Libenson decided to found C2C Tutors after hearing stories of their friends and family being vastly overcharged for subpar or simple tutoring services. After successfully mentoring their peers who utilized professional tutors, Ethan and Jake realized they could be better resources at a lower price than existing professional tutors services.

Ethan is a student at Harvard university while Jake attends UC Berkeley. Both have a proven track record of success across all disciplines from academics, to clubs, to athletics, to internships. They founded C2C Tutors with the absolute greatest priority of allowing high schoolers to maximize their potential in a feasible and personable manner.
